## Hires on the bigsign

`nyancat`, `pokeball`, and `baseball` auto-activate hi-res sprites when the canvas is at scale > 1 (the bigsign). The hi-res variants are noticeably more detailed and read better at distance:

- **nyancat** — animated webp Nyan Cat sprite at native physical resolution
- **pokeball** — procedural ball with animated Pikachu run-cycle sprite
- **baseball** — procedural ball reusing the `:baseball:` emoji geometry, 8 rotation frames

`sailor_moon` and `pacman` keep their 8-bit look at all scales — the chunky aesthetic is part of the design.
